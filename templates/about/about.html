Policy

In short, don't spam me. My voicemail is scam-filled already, so don't make it worse.

In particular:

  • Should go without saying: Do not solicit/spam me. I'll probably block you (on community lists).
  • twonum.org is my personal website. It would be meaningless if it was filled with others' posts on DoubleFloat, so I do not accept guest posts.
  • Don't promote your job offering/personal beliefs. My current arrangements are good enough. (Sharing interesting stuff is okay; don't convert me/try to hire me).
  • git@twonum.org is only used for Git commits. Don't use it for comms.
  • If I gave express permission before, it overrides this policy, but remember that they're revocable.
  • Use the DANE OPENPGPKEY record or Web Key Directory to receive my OpenPGP key. (Encrypting with my key lessens spam chances; no bots use PGP!)
  • All emails are read in plain text (HTML email is known to be bad). Don't use fancy HTML. Use plain text email if possible (on Thunderbird, it's just Shift + New Message), otherwise don't format your HTML.

After reading my policy: Why do you want my email?

I want to sell you something

Don't.

I will block you.

I want to change your personal beliefs or hire you, not just share interesting stuff

My inbox is not a soapbox.

I will delete your email. Repeat offenders will get blocked.

I want to share something interesting/ask about something else/just say hi

You absolutely can email me, fellow stranger!
